MLSA AI PROJECT WINNER


Winner of the prestigious Microsoft MLSA AI Project award Collaborative effort by a global team from the USA, Australia, and India Innovative use of Azure AI services and Unity Engine to create an immersive learning experience


Street Savvy is an innovative, interactive solution designed to educate individuals about Indian traffic rules, leveraging the power of Azure AI services and Unity Engine. This award-winning project, developed by a collaborative team from the USA, Australia, and India, aims to address the prevalent problems of traffic education by providing an engaging, cost-effective, and personalized learning experience. Key Features: Engaging gamified simulation to educate users about Indian traffic rules Built-in AI assistant providing personalized guidance and teaching traffic rules Instant answers to questions about specific traffic rules using Azure AI Bot services Conversational AI powered by Azure AI Speech for voice interaction Real-life scenarios to simulate on-road experiences Cost-effective alternative to traditional driver education Interactive learning tool for children to learn about traffic signals and rules
